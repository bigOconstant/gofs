Faq

September 16, 2021

  1. Do you support hate speech?

    • We support all speech, because the line between discussion and hate is not easily defined. Don’t like a post? downvote, move on.

  2. Where can I post content?

    • wolfballs.com

  3. Do you allow porn?

    • We do not currently have a policy against it that may be revisited if it is a problem. We do not have much manpower to moderate that and it’s to easy for illegal content to be slipped in.

  4. What kinda speech is allowed?

    • All legal speech. We reserve the right to delete content that is obviously planted by governments, competing companies, or bots. Any content intented to actually further a conversation will not be removed.

  5. Why did you start with lemmy? Aren’t those guys commies?

    • It’s not productive to build anything from scratch. We beleive in the ideals of federation, democratic development and the sharing of code. While we lean more liberarian here, populism is key to our collective freedom. We will fight our fight and respect their code liscenses.
